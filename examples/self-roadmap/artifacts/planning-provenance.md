# Planning provenance

This OSS demo intentionally does not depend on the original private planning
documents or a-thread transcripts.

The planning nodes in `features/planning/framework-planning.yaml` are retained as
historical context for why the self-roadmap is shaped this way. They are capped at
`heuristic` proof in this repository until the real PRD/spec/review artifacts are
published here.

Do not promote these nodes to `unit`, `owning_e2e`, or `destination` without adding
the corresponding local artifact and pointing `coverage.evidenceRef` at it.
